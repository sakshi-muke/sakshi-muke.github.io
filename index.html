Cupcakes

A cupcake is a small, individual-sized cake designed to serve one person. Made with similar ingredients as traditional cakes, such as flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, cupcakes are baked in a muffin tin and often topped with frosting, sprinkles, or other decorations. They come in a variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to more creative combinations like red velvet, lemon, and salted caramel. Cupcakes are perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or as a sweet treat for any occasion, offering both convenience and indulgence in every bite.
